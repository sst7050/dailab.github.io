Welcome to the DAI LAB

Data analysis & Artificial Intelligence laboratory,

department of Computer engineering, Chungbuk National University, South Korea. We are focusing on two techniques: deep learning and topic modeling.
Lab's Interests

Our laboratory focuses on the following research and development areas.

NLP

chatbot, dialog system, information extraction

Image Processing

object recognition, image classification

Security

malware detection

Clinical data analysis

prediction of emergency, post-operative outcome

Pharmaceutic data analysis

formation of coformer pairs
